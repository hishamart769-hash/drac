About the Hudawi Course

The Hudawi Course is a 12-year integrated Islamic and academic education program under Darul Huda Islamic University.

It includes deep learning in Quran, Hadith, Fiqh, Arabic, along with modern education and soft skills. Students are trained to be leaders and educators with moral, intellectual, and social excellence.
